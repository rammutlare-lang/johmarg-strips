ABOUT US

Johmarg Strips is a South African manufacturer and supplier of premium tile trims, profiles and flooring accessories. Since 2023, we have been committed to delivering high quality solutions that combine precision, durability and style to complete every project with excellence.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2023

Founded in 2023, Johmarg Strips started with a simple mission – to supply high quality finishing profiles that add the perfect touch to tiles and flooring.

Over the years, we have grown through dedication, product innovation and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction. Today, we are trusted by contractors, architects, builders and homeowners across South Africa for products that are built to perform and made to last.
